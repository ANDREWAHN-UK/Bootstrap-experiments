Tainted Grail

Following on from the massive success of Gloomhaven, there follwed several games that can only be described as....big. Tainted Grail is among the biggest of them all, promising some truly epic gameplay. Well, to call it divisive would be simplifying things. Like your fingers touching the Sistine Chapel, it comes close to greatness on occasion, but never consistently manages to make things FUN.
